About Vijay Karnatak

Mr. Vijay Karnatak


is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at THDC Institute of Hydropower Engineering and Technology, Tehri, Uttarakhand. He is Pursuing Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering from Graphic Era Deemed to be University, holding a background of M.Tech. and B.Tech. from Uttarakhand Technical University.

His expertise lies in Cloud Computing & Machine Learning and IoT, demonstrated through 10+ research papers in prestigious conferences and journals. He also holds three Indian Patents in Machine Learning. He actively participates and serves as a reviewer for renowned journals. In education, he contributes through Faculty Development Programs and expert talks at prestigious universities. His dedication to academia earned him the "Best Faculty Award" at the GECL-2022. With a career spanning 5.5+ years, Mr. Vijay Karnatak has worked at various institutions, including MIET, Glocal University, Chandigarh University and Coer University.

He is an active member of professional organizations likeI2OR. Mr. Vijay Karnatak is a distinguished figure in the field of computer science and engineering.
